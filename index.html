About Lakshadweep

Explore the charm of Lakshadweep, a group of picturesque islands in the Arabian Sea. With its coral reefs, lagoons, and unique culture, Lakshadweep is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered.

Lakshadweep, meaning 'a hundred thousand islands' in Sanskrit, is a group of 36 mesmerizing coral islands in the Arabian Sea. These islands are known for their pristine beauty, rich marine life, and unique culture. Explore the charm and serenity of Lakshadweep.

Top Places to Visit

Kavaratti Island

Experience the beauty of Kavaratti, the capital of Lakshadweep, with its stunning beaches and vibrant marine life. Snorkel in the clear waters and explore the coral reefs.

Agatti Island

Agatti Island is known for its coral-rich lagoons and water sports. Enjoy scuba diving, kayaking, and relax on the sandy shores surrounded by coconut palms.

Activities

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and exciting activities of Lakshadweep:

  • Snorkeling in crystal-clear lagoons
  • Scuba diving to explore coral reefs
  • Island hopping and boat tours
  • Traditional dance performances
  • Relaxing on pristine beaches
